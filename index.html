Level Ball Format
The Level Ball Format is a brand new way of playing the Pokemon TCG. A fun deck-building puzzle and a way to highlight some under-utilised cards, the Level Ball Format brings some restrictions to the cards you can play with and the way decks are built. Below you can find the key rules of the format, some notable support for each type in the game and some example archetypes for you to try out!
Rules:
  • Only Pokemon with 90HP or less can be used.
  • You may only use 3 cards of the same name in your deck (instead of the usual 4).
  • You may only use cards indicated from F-On (Scarlet and Violet onwards).
Banned Cards:
  • Paradise Resort
  • Luxurious Cape
