Level Ball Format
A fun deck-building puzzle and a way to highlight some under-utilised cards, the Level Ball Format brings some restrictions to the cards you can play with and the way decks are built!
Rules
You may only use Pokemon with 90HP or less.
You may only use 3 cards of the same name in your deck (instead of the usual 4).
You may only use cards indicated from F-On (Scarlet and Violet onwards)
